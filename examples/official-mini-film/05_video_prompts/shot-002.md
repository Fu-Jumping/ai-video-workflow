# Shot 002 Video Prompt

A single continuous shot on the same empty riverside walkway at night. The courier slows to a stop beneath a flickering streetlight beside the low metal railing. The right hand lifts the small sealed data card closer to the chest for a brief check, catching a pale flash from the unstable light. The wet pavement reflects the flicker in broken patches, and the dark river remains visible beyond the railing. The camera holds a restrained medium view with a slight push-in. No new characters appear, no location change occurs, and the movement stays quiet and controlled.
