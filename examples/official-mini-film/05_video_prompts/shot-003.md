# Shot 003 Video Prompt

A single continuous shot beneath the riverside overpass before sunrise. The courier steps into position in front of a small dark metal service door and raises the sealed data card toward the narrow reader beside the door. The movement is slow and precise. Damp concrete reflects a faint cool edge light, while the overpass structure stays heavy and dark above the frame. The camera holds a steady medium-wide angle from behind and slightly to the side, keeping the card, reader, and door visible. No new characters enter, and the shot ends before the door opens.
