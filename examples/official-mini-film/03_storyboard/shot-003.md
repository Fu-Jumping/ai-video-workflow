# Shot 003

## Visible Description

The courier reaches the small metal service door beneath the riverside overpass. The sealed data card is raised toward a narrow door reader. Damp concrete walls and the underside of the overpass frame the figure, while a faint pre-sunrise glow begins to appear beyond the river edge.
