# Scene Profile

## Riverside Walkway

- Empty urban riverside walkway at night.
- Wet pavement reflects pale streetlights.
- Low railing separates the walkway from dark water.
- One flickering streetlight creates an unstable pool of light near the railing.
- A small metal service door sits under the riverside overpass at the end of the walkway.
- Distant apartment towers create small reflected points of light.
