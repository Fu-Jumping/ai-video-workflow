# Shot 002 Keyframe

## 快速导读

夜晚河边步道的闪烁路灯下，穿深色连帽雨衣的快递员停在低矮栏杆旁，右手把小型密封数据卡贴近胸前，湿地面反射不稳定的冷白灯光。

## 中文完整版本

画面是夜晚的城市河边步道中段。前景是湿润的深灰色路面，路面反射一盏闪烁街灯形成的断续冷白光斑；中景是一名成年快递员，穿深色连帽雨衣，站在低矮金属栏杆旁，身体略微转向画面左侧，右手把一张小型浅色密封数据卡贴近胸前。人物姿态静止、谨慎，脸部仍被雨衣帽檐遮住一部分。画面右侧是栏杆和暗色河水，背景有稀疏的远处楼灯。主要材质是潮湿路面、金属栏杆、防水布料和浅色数据卡。

避免: 白天场景、多人出现、奔跑动作、数据卡变大或消失、强烈霓虹招牌、正面大特写、室内走廊。

## English Version (Copy Ready)

A night scene on the middle section of an urban riverside walkway. The foreground shows dark wet pavement reflecting broken cool-white patches from a flickering streetlight. In the middle ground, an adult courier wearing a dark hooded rain jacket stands beside a low metal railing, body turned slightly toward the left side of the frame. The courier holds a small pale sealed data card close to the chest with the right hand. The posture is still and cautious, and the hood partially shadows the face. On the right side of the frame, the railing and dark river water remain visible. Sparse apartment lights appear in the far background. The key materials are wet pavement, metal railing, waterproof fabric, and the small pale data card.

Avoid: daytime lighting, multiple people, running action, oversized or missing data card, strong neon signs, frontal close-up, indoor corridor.
